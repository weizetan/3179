Redefining HAPPINESS in 2022

Welcome to the 2022 World Happiness Report. This report explores happiness worldwide. It looks at how happy different countries are and why. The measurement is based on factors like money, support from others, health, freedom, fairness, and kindness. Governments and organizations use this information to make decisions that can improve people's lives. So, let's dive into the happiness of the world in 2022!

It's interesting to observe that Finland leads among those countries with the highest happiness score.

Viewers have a chance to filter data based on certain criteria by using the legend. The graph will update appropriately as a result. By carefully examining the graph above, we can see that GDP per capita is within the greatest range, indicating that it has a considerable impact on how the happiness score is determined.
